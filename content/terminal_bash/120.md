# strftime - format date and time

## Presets Date
%m/%d/%Y 	06/05/2013
%A, %B %e, %Y 	Sunday, June 5, 2013
%b %e %a 	Jun 5 Sun
Time
%H:%M 	23:05
%I:%M %p 	11:05 PM

## Date
%a 	Sun 	Weekday
%A 	Sunday 	 
%w 	0..6 (Sunday is 0) 	 
%y 	13 	Year
%Y 	2013 	 
%b 	Jan 	Month
%B 	January 	 
%m 	01..12 	 
%d 	01..31 	Day
%e 	1..31 	 

## Time
%l 	1 	Hour
%H 	00..23 	24h Hour
%I 	01..12 	12h Hour
%M 	00..59 	Minute
%S 	00..60 	Second
%p 	AM 	AM or PM
%Z 	+08 	Time zone
%j 	001..366 	Day of the year
%% 	% 	Literal % character
